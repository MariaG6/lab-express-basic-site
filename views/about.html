David Goggins

About David

Billed as "the world's toughest man alive," David Goggins is a retired NAVY SEAL veteran of the Afghanistan and Iran wars who runs ultramarathons, long-distance cycling, is a triathlete and holds the world record for more pull-ups (dominated) in 24 hours (achieved 4,030 pull-ups in 17 hours). Goggins, who has a legion of followers on his social networks and who succeeds with his motivational talks, does not hesitate to show his hard training methods.
